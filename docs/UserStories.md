##  User Stories  

**EPIC 1: Customer Insights Dashboard**  
- **As the Director of Customer Retention**, I want to view churn metrics so that I can monitor overall retention performance and make strategic decisions.  
-**Acceptance Criteria** : The dashboard must display overall churn rate trends over time with filters by region, plan type, and tenure to support strategic review.

- **As a Marketing Team member**, I want to see churn drivers so that I can design targeted retention campaigns.    
-**Acceptance Criteria** : The dashboard must highlight top churn drivers with segment-level breakdowns (e.g., contract type, usage, complaints) to enable targeted campaigns.

- **As a Product Team member**, I want to identify which service bundles are linked to churn so that we can improve offerings.  
-**Acceptance Criteria** : The dashboard must show churn rates by service bundle and feature combination to identify underperforming offerings.

- **As a Finance Team member**, I want to track revenue impact of churn so that I can assess financial risks.  
-**Acceptance Criteria** : The dashboard must calculate and display revenue lost due to churn and projected revenue at risk by customer segment.



**EPIC 2: Alerts & Monitoring**  
- **As the Director of Customer Retention**, I want customers segmented by churn risk level so that proactive retention planning and prioritization can be performed.                   
-**Acceptance Criteria** : Customers must be classified into churn risk levels (High, Medium, Low) based on defined churn indicators and displayed with segment-level counts and trends.

- **As a Marketing Team member**, I want churn-risk–based customer segments so that targeted retention campaigns can be designed for high-risk groups.     
-**Acceptance Criteria** : The system must provide churn-risk–based customer segments with key attributes (plan, tenure, usage) to support targeted retention campaign planning.

- **As a Product Team member**, I want churn trends analyzed by tenure and service features so that product improvement opportunities can be evaluated.      
-**Acceptance Criteria** : The dashboard must display churn trends segmented by customer tenure and service features to enable evaluation of product performance and improvement areas.
