# Business Requirements Document (BRD)
**Project:** Telecom Customer Churn Analysis  
**Role:** Business Analyst (solo project)

---

## 1. Executive Summary
A telecom company is losing 27% of its customers annually, causing revenue loss and higher acquisition costs.  
The goal is to understand why customers leave, identify churn drivers, and implement proactive retention measures.

---

## 2. Business Need
- Reduce customer churn and revenue loss.
- Identify key churn drivers.
- Improve internal retention workflows.
- Monitor churn trends using a dashboard.

---

## 3. Current State Challenges
- No structured churn monitoring.
- Reactive retention processes.
- Siloed customer information.
- Limited insights into high-risk customers.

---

## 4. Project Scope
**In Scope:**
- Analyze churn drivers using SQL and Tableau.
- Create a dashboard with churn metrics.
- Propose retention strategies.

**Out of Scope:**
- Changing Backend system.
- Marketing campaign execution.

---

## 5. Success Metrics
- Reduce churn by 10% within 6 months.
- Automate daily churn reporting.
- Identify high-risk customers early for intervention.

---

## 6. Stakeholders
- Sales Manager
- Retention Specialist
- IT / Data Team
- Executive Leadership

---

## 8. Constraints & Risks
- Limited data availability for certain segments.
- Dependence on call center adoption of new workflow.
- Accuracy of customer engagement data.
- Resource limitations for implementing retention campaigns.
