# Telecom-Customer-Churn-BA-Project
End-to-end Business Analysis project analyzing telecom customer churn using SQL and Tableau.


## Business Context
The telecom industry operates in a highly competitive market where customers have numerous alternatives for service providers. The company is experiencing significant customer churn, resulting in revenue loss and higher costs for acquiring new customers.

Leadership is under pressure to identify which customer segments, service offerings, or subscription behaviors are driving churn. A clear understanding of these factors is required to implement targeted retention strategies, optimize service bundles, and improve overall customer lifetime value.

By analyzing churn drivers and patterns, decision-makers can prioritize interventions that reduce revenue leakage and strengthen customer loyalty, ensuring sustainable growth in a competitive market.

## Problem Statement
A telecom company is losing 27% of its customers annually, causing revenue loss and higher acquisition costs. Leadership wants to understand why customers leave, identify churn drivers, improve retention, and create a dashboard for monitoring.

## Stakeholders
Primary Stakeholder:
- Director of Customer Retention, responsible for reducing churn and protecting recurring revenue.

Secondary Stakeholders:
– Marketing team, focused on retention campaigns and personalized offers
– Product team, responsible for service bundles and customer experience
– Finance team, monitoring revenue impact and customer lifetime value

## Business Objectives
- Reduce customer churn and mitigate revenue loss by identifying high-risk segments and churn drivers.
- Strengthen customer retention by analyzing service usage and subscription patterns to pinpoint areas needing improvement.
- Enable stakeholders to prioritize retention initiatives by providing actionable insights into the most impactful interventions.

## Success Metrics (KPIs)
- Churn Rate (%) — overall and segmented by service, contract type, and customer demographics
- Revenue at Risk — potential revenue loss from high-risk customers
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) — average value of retained vs churned customers
- Retention Campaign Effectiveness — % of high-risk customers retained after targeted campaigns
